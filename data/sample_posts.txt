Sample Health Content for Testing MedTrust AI

Sample 1 — High Risk
Claim:
This herbal drink cures diabetes in 7 days without medicine.

Expected behavior:
Flag as high risk because it claims a cure for a chronic condition without medical evidence.

Sample 2 — High Risk
Claim:
Avoid all vaccines because natural immunity is always better.

Expected behavior:
Flag as high risk because it discourages evidence-based public health guidance.

Sample 3 — Low Risk
Claim:
Drinking water in the morning supports hydration and digestion, but it is not a cure for disease.

Expected behavior:
Flag as low risk because the claim is general wellness advice and avoids exaggerated medical promises.

Sample 4 — High Risk
Claim:
This weight loss tea burns belly fat overnight without diet or exercise.

Expected behavior:
Flag as high risk because it promotes unrealistic rapid weight-loss claims.

Sample 5 — Safe Medical Guidance
Claim:
Always consult a qualified healthcare professional before stopping prescribed medication.

Expected behavior:
Flag as safe guidance because it encourages professional medical consultation.

Sample 6 — Medium Risk
Claim:
Carbs are the main reason people develop diabetes and obesity.

Expected behavior:
Flag as medium risk because the claim oversimplifies a complex medical issue.

Sample 7 — Medium Risk
Claim:
ADHD is not caused by laziness, but social media self-checklists cannot confirm a diagnosis.

Expected behavior:
Flag as medium risk or low risk depending on context, while reminding users that diagnosis requires professional evaluation.
